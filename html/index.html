01 / ABOUT

The Intersection

I'm Brady — a Computer Science and Statistics student at Eastern Kentucky University with a deep obsession for college basketball strategy and analytics. I sit at a rare intersection: technical enough to build the tools, knowledgeable enough to know what actually matters on the court.

My focus is building analytics tools for college basketball — specifically surfacing the kind of data-driven insight that used to require a full analytics staff. The WPI model, Stats Lab, and Baseline Logic are all part of that mission.

Building in public. Learning out loud. Rooted in Richmond, Kentucky.
